Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Statedge, we collect the following information:

  • TikTok Account Information: Username, profile information, and account ID when you connect your TikTok account
  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens to authenticate with TikTok on your behalf
  • Content Data: Videos and captions you upload for scheduling
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Service, including scheduled posts and publish times

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
  • Schedule and publish content to your TikTok account as instructed by you
  • Maintain and improve the Service
  • Communicate with you about the Service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest and in transit. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • TikTok: We share data with TikTok through their official API to publish content on your behalf
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. OAuth tokens are automatically refreshed or deleted based on TikTok's token expiration policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Disconnect your TikTok account at any time

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and provide the Service. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with TikTok. Please review TikTok's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the Service.
